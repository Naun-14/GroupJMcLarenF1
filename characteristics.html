McLaren: Characteristics

About McLaren

McLaren is a British automotive manufacturer and Formula One team. Founded in 1963 by Bruce McLaren, the company is known for pushing the boundaries of technology and motorsport innovation.

Key Characteristics of McLaren

  • Founded: 1963
  • Founder: Bruce McLaren
  • Headquarters: Woking, England
  • Industry: Formula One and high-performance automotive
  • Notable For: Advanced materials, carbon fiber use, hybrid systems, aerodynamic excellence
  • Formula One Debut: 1966 (first race: 1966 Monaco Grand Prix)
  • Famous Cars: McLaren F1, McLaren P1, McLaren 720S, McLaren 570S, McLaren MP4/4
  • Racing Achievements: 8 Constructors' Championships, 12 Drivers' Championships in Formula One
  • Notable Drivers: Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, Mika Häkkinen, Lewis Hamilton, Daniel Ricciardo, Lando Norris
  • Key Partnerships: TAG (engine supplier), Honda, Mercedes-Benz, Renault, and more recently, McLaren's own power unit department
